About Us

Bezond Hied Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 27 January 2020. It is classified as a Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Kanpur. It is involved in the Manufacture of other chemical products and preparations. Bezond Hied Private Limited is dedicated to producing high-quality chemical products that meet the needs of various industries. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, the company aims to be a leader in the market. Their commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction sets them apart from the competition. By prioritizing quality control and utilizing cutting-edge technology, Bezond Hied Private Limited continues to make a positive impact in the chemical manufacturing industry.
